Lemon Chicken Sghetti

Getchu Some of that Tasty Sghetti and Butter!

Sghetti, butter, and lemons. What more do you need?

Ingredients

  • 10 oz chicken cutlets
  • 6 oz spaghetti
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Chicken stock concentrate
  • 2 tbsp sour cream
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Wash and dry produce
  3. Trim and quarter zucchini lengthwise; cut crosswise into 1/2 inch thick pieces
  4. Peel and mince garlic
  5. Zest and quarter lemon
  6. Once water is boiling, add spaghetti to pot, stirring occasionally, until al dente, for 9-11 minutes
  7. Reserve 1 cup pasta cooking water, then drain
  8. While pasta cooks, heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  9. Add zucchini; cook, stirring occasionally, until browned and softened, for 4-6 minutes
  10. Season zucchini with salt and pepper
  11. Turn off heat, transfer zucchini to a plate. Wipe out the pan
  12. While zucchini is cooking, pat chicken ddry and season all over with italian seasoning, salt, and pepper
  13. Heat large drizzle of olive oil in wiped pan over medium-high heat
  14. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, 3-5 minutes per side
  15. Turn off heat and transfer to a cutting board to rest
  16. Once cool enough to handle, slice chicken crosswise
  17. Heat drizzle of olive oil in the pot used for spaghetti over medium-high heat
  18. Add garlic, half the lemon zest, and a pinch of chili flakes. Cook, stirring, until fragrant, 20-30 seconds
  19. Stir in 1/2 cup reserved pasta cooking water, stock concentrate, and juice from two lemon wedges
  20. Simmer until thickened, 1-2 minutes
  21. Turn off heat
  22. Add drained spaghetti, zucchini, sour cream, and 1 tbsp of butter to pot with sauce. Toss to coat
  23. Add half of the parmesan and season with salt and pepper
  24. Divide pasta between bowls
  25. Top with chicken, remaining parmesan, remaining lemon zest, and a pinch of chili flakes if desired
  26. Serve with any remaining lemon wedges on the side
